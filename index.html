About

I’m Meng Wang, a fifth-year PhD student at Department of Computer Science at University of Chicago, advised by Prof. Haryadi S. Gunawi. I'm part of UCARE group. Prior to this, I got my bachelor degree in Industrial Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016, and master degree in Computer Science from The University of Chicago in 2017.

I’m interested in Operating and Storage systems, Distributed Cloud Systems, and Systems for Machine Learning. My research focuses on improving the availability and reliability of cloud storage systems, and building/improving storage systems for machine learning
